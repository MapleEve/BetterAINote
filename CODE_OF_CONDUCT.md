# 行为准则

[English](#english)

## 中文

请以尊重、直接、建设性的方式协作。

### 我们鼓励

- 清晰表达问题与证据
- 讨论方案时聚焦事实
- 尊重不同背景和经验

### 我们不接受

- 人身攻击
- 歧视或骚扰
- 故意提交破坏性内容

## English

Please collaborate in a respectful, direct, and constructive way.

### Encouraged

- clear problem statements with evidence
- fact-based technical discussion
- respect for different backgrounds and experience levels

### Not accepted

- personal attacks
- discrimination or harassment
- intentionally destructive submissions
